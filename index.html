WHO WE ARE

A startup validation studio built for Vietnam's blue oceans.

We are on a mission to build 20 blue-ocean companies over 10 years. We originate ideas internally, test them with structured experiments, invest in the winners, and operate them alongside founders.

WHAT WE DO

We kill ideas for a living.
The ones that survive become companies.

We pick massive problems, design experiments to test every assumption, and only invest in the ideas that survive. Most don't. That is our point.

Every framework, every failed experiment, every honest result goes public. If it saves one founder three months, the work was worth it.

5 BLUE OCEAN SECTORS

Where we're looking. And why.

Five sectors in Vietnam where real problems hide inside broken systems.
We are researching across blue ocean markets.

Education

Vietnam's system rewards memorization over imagination. We're building for discovery and critical thinking in the next generation.

Insurance

Fragmented, opaque, inaccessible. We're designing transparent, tech-enabled protection that people actually trust.

Urban

Cities are growing faster than their systems. We're solving daily friction in transport, housing, and services.

Health

The gap between how long people live and how well they live keeps widening. We're shifting toward prevention and long-term wellness.

Culture

Decades of rapid growth left a cultural foundation underdeveloped. We're investing in cultural literacy and national confidence.
